Tomato farming Nepal

Commercial srijana verity tomato farming Nepal is going popular day by day between Nepali farmers. Tomato production or can be grown in between 10 C – 30 C of Nepal, only frost free areas almost throughout the year. What do you do when you are in need of tomatoes? Simple, you visit the market and buy some dozens of tomato. But, did you know that the best way of getting high-quality tomato is by growing it yourself? Well, it is true. Organic tomatoes are the best when it comes to health facts. You might get some high-quality tomato in the supermarket itself, but it will not be half as good as the ones you grow yourself in your farm.

Tips to remember while you are farming tomatoes organically, You will be hooked as soon as you tuck a vine-ripened tomato. Tomatoes require long growing season with about 6 to 12 hours of sun each day. Sumner heat is best to ripen a tomato. The rule of growing tomatoes are larger the tomatoes are, more heat it is going to require. Cherry tomatoes are the ones who need the least amount of heat. Try to grow them in the sunniest or the warmest corner of the garden, For excellent performance.

Tomatoes are considered to be summer vegetables. Thus, they die at the first sight of frost. Do not make the mistake of setting out tomatoes farming Nepal just before the last day of frost in your area. Gardens which are in the southern and northern them is sphere start seeding it 6 to 8 weeks before frost happens. You must plant the seedling outside after the dangerous frost has passed.

Tomatoes thrive best in soils which have a lot of organic matter. Basically, it requires a deep zone for its roots. Garden beds which are raised are best for tomato plantation. You must also vertically train them for best results. Deep and loose loam’s are best for the growth of tomatoes. Try and build the fertilizers on the ground if you are growing them organically.
