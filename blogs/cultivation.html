Off-Season vegetable cultivation in Nepal

Off-season cultivation of vegetable refers to the production of the vegetable before or after their normal cultivation seasons of production. This can be accomplished by using the different agro-climatic conditions, adjusting the planting time, selecting and improving the varieties or by creating the controlled environment by making the plastic tunnels, polythene houses, permanent glass houses etc.

Advantages of the off-seasons vegetable cultivation

  • Off-seasons vegetable offers the higher prices to the farmers.
  • Consumers may have the fresh vegetable even in the off-seasons. Farmeres can get the employment round the year.
  • Farmers can increase their knowledge so that they can adopt the vegetable cultivation in the commercial scale.
  • Off-seasons vegetable production has a lot of prospects for the exports in the foreign country so that there is ample scope of hard earned curriciencies.

Disadvantages of off-seasons vegetables

  • It needs skilled knowledge than the normal seasons production
  • Production cost of vegetable is higher.
  • It's is riskier business as there is always threat of insects , pests and diseases .
  • Off-seasons production can be adopted in commercial if is near by the market.

Our country, Nepal is located in the geographical of latitude of 27.9389°N and longitude of 84.9408°E and is endowed with the varied agro-climatic zones. Location at altitude varing from 400 to 2000 meters are considered suitable for the off-season vegetable production. According to the Nepal Agriculture Research Council(NARC) off season production technologies for tomato, onion, cucumber,cabbage,and cauliflower have been developed and recommended to farmers in these reasons. Kathmandu, Pokhara, Chitwan and so on where there is high concentration of business and the government activities as well as tourism is also flourished. Panchkal(Kabre), Tistung, Palung, Daman(Makwanpur), Ranipauwa(Nuwakot) , Basantapur, Hile,Sidhuwa(Dhankuta), Madanpokhara(Palpa), Dhading and many pocket along the east-west highway are important location where farmershave successfully grown of the off-season vegetables. Like wise the farmers group of Ratanangla- Dailekh, Kapurkot- Salyan ,Sindhuwa-Dhankuta , working independently or with the help of the some existing agriculture related institution for expansion of the off-season vegetable production.
